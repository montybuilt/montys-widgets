Login Pseudocode

State transitions:
STATE: Logged Out → click "Login" → STATE: Entered Credentials
↓ (submit valid password) ↓
STATE: Logged In
↓ (invalid password) ↓
STATE: Logged Out
STATE: Logged In → click "Logout" → STATE: Logged Out
